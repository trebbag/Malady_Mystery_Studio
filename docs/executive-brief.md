# Executive brief

## What this program is

A governed AI content platform that turns diseases into comic-style clinical mysteries while meeting commercial-grade standards for quality, auditability, and operational control.

## Why it is not a “prompt app”

The platform must simultaneously satisfy:
- provider-level medical correctness,
- entertaining mystery storytelling,
- comics-grade panelization,
- enterprise reliability and governance.

That requires a pipeline, not a single prompt.

## Commercial implications

This product can support:
- medical education publishers,
- health-system education teams,
- CME/teaching products,
- internal training programs,
- specialty content studios.

## Program success criteria

A successful first commercial release should demonstrate:
- repeatable generation across a curated disease set,
- measurable eval performance,
- human-review workflows,
- versioned prompts and schemas,
- auditable release bundles.

## Recommended staffing nucleus

- product lead
- tech lead / staff engineer
- full-stack engineer
- platform/infra engineer
- prompt/evals engineer
- clinician reviewer
- editorial lead / narrative designer
- art pipeline lead

## Recommended sequencing

1. foundation and governance
2. clinical intake and evidence layer
3. mystery workbook engine
4. scene and panel planner
5. render prep and export
6. review console and release operations

## Biggest avoidable mistake

Trying to ship the narrative generator before the contracts, evals, and review gates exist.
