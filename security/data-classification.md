# Data classification (initial)

## Data classes

### Public
- published marketing material
- public product documentation

### Internal
- backlog items
- internal prompts
- eval datasets without sensitive content
- architecture documents

### Restricted
- customer-specific configuration
- reviewer identities and audit trails
- unpublished content bundles
- any patient-like or PHI-adjacent data if future integrations introduce it

## Policy intent

Assume future enterprise customers will require clearer segregation, retention controls, and auditability than an internal prototype would normally receive.
