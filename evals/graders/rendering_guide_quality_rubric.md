# Rendering-guide-quality grader rubric

Check:
- provider-specific prompt blocks exist for every panel,
- Nano Banana prompts begin with explicit image creation language and keep text out of the art request,
- Genspark instructions are one-panel-per-slide, sequential, and style-locked from slide 1,
- lettering overlays are explicit and editable,
- clinically important panels preserve claim traceability and continuity/anatomy locks.

Automatic fail examples:
- missing Nano Banana prompt or Genspark slide block for any panel,
- no explicit lettering separation,
- no deck bootstrap prompt,
- Genspark instructions that allow live research or parallel slide generation,
- clinically material panels with no claim references.
